Ethical Hacking Workshop mainly focuses on the students eager to be a White Hat Hacker. It features a brief introduction to the world of hacking starting with the importance of ethical hackers and their need in today’s world scenario. This workshop will help learn what is ethical hacking, it focuses on:
-Android Hacking
-Windows RAT
-Phishing
-SQL Injection
-Steganography
Students with personal laptops will be guided in installing VMWare and downloading the ISO files. After brief introduction on Ethical Hacking the students will be given a hands on experience in the above listed domains.
